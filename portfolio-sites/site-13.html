Craftsmanship Rooted in Tradition

Heritage Brickwork was established in 1995 by master craftsman Robert Simmonds. Based in the heart of Oxford, we specialise in both period restoration and modern construction, bringing the same meticulous attention to detail to every project.

Our team of eight experienced bricklayers has worked on listed buildings, college restorations, and contemporary new builds across Oxfordshire. We understand the unique requirements of working in a city with such rich architectural heritage.

Our Expertise

Traditional skills for modern and period properties

🏛

Period Restoration

Sensitive restoration of listed and period buildings using traditional materials and techniques. We work with conservation officers to ensure all work meets heritage requirements.

🏠

Residential Extensions

Beautiful extensions that complement your existing property. Careful brick matching and attention to architectural detail.

🧱

Decorative Brickwork

Ornamental features, corbelling, arches, and decorative bond patterns. We bring artistic flair to functional brickwork, creating beautiful focal points.

🔧

Lime Mortar Repointing

Specialist repointing using traditional lime mortars for period properties. We match existing mortar profiles and colours for an authentic finish.

🏗

New Build Projects

Complete brickwork for new residential and commercial developments. We deliver consistent quality across projects of all sizes.

🏡

Garden Features

Walls, pillars, barbecues, and bespoke garden structures. Crafted to enhance your outdoor living space.
