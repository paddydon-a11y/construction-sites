Building Excellence in Bath

Bridgewater Builders was established in 2010 by directors Steven Carr and James McNeil. With a combined 40 years of experience in the construction industry, they set out to create a building company that prioritises quality, communication, and reliability.

Based in Bath, we specialise in high-end residential construction, sympathetic renovations of period properties, and bespoke new builds. Our team of 35 skilled tradesmen and project managers ensures every project is delivered to the highest standards.
