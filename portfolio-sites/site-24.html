The Art of Garden Design

Eden Garden Design creates exquisite outdoor spaces for discerning homeowners across North Yorkshire. Led by principal designer Katherine Stirling, our studio brings together horticultural expertise, architectural sensitivity, and an unwavering commitment to quality. Every garden we design is a unique response to its setting, its owners, and the landscape that surrounds it.

Our Design Process

01

Initial Consultation

We visit your property to understand your aspirations, assess the site and discuss the scope and budget for your garden project.

02

Concept Design

We develop initial design concepts with mood boards, sketch plans and material samples that capture the vision for your garden.

03

Detailed Design

Full technical drawings, planting plans, lighting schemes and construction specifications are prepared for the build team.

04

Construction & Planting

We oversee every stage of construction with our trusted landscaping partners, ensuring the design is realised exactly as envisioned.

05

Aftercare & Maturation

We provide a detailed maintenance guide and seasonal garden care visits to ensure your garden flourishes for years to come.
