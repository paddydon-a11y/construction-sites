Craftsmanship in Every Stone

For over thirty years, Cotswold Stonework has been shaping, carving, and building with natural Cotswold limestone. From drystone boundary walls to intricate carved fireplaces, we bring skill, patience, and pride to every commission. Based in Cirencester, we serve clients throughout the Cotswolds and beyond.

Our Craft

Traditional stonemasonry services for new builds, restorations, and bespoke commissions.

▢

Drystone Walling

Traditional Cotswold drystone walls built and repaired by hand using locally sourced limestone. Boundary walls, retaining walls, and garden features.

❖

Stone Carving

Bespoke stone carving for architectural features, house name plates, memorials, and decorative elements. Hand-carved to your specification by our master craftsmen.

■

New Build Stonework

Natural stone building for extensions, new homes, and outbuildings in traditional Cotswold style. Working in partnership with architects and builders.

✿

Heritage Restoration

Sympathetic restoration of period stonework on listed buildings, churches, and historic properties. Lime mortar pointing, stone indent repairs, and like-for-like replacement.

○

Fireplaces & Surrounds

Hand-carved stone fireplaces, surrounds, and hearths in natural Cotswold stone. From simple inglenook styles to elaborate period designs.

♢

Garden Stonework

Stone patios, pathways, steps, raised beds, and water features. Creating beautiful outdoor spaces with natural materials that complement the landscape.
