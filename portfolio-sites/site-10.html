About K&S

K&S Bricklaying is a family-run business established in 2008. Founded by brothers Kevin and Steve Marsh, we've been laying bricks across Brighton and the surrounding Sussex area for over 17 years.

We take pride in honest work, fair prices, and doing the job right first time. From a single garden wall to a full two-storey extension, we treat every project with the same care and attention to detail.

  • ✓ Fully insured — public liability up to £5m
  • ✓ NHBC registered & CSCS card holders
  • ✓ City & Guilds qualified bricklayers
  • ✓ References available on request

What We Do

Quality bricklaying services for homes across Sussex

House Extensions

Single and double-storey extensions built to building regs. We handle all the brickwork and blockwork, coordinating with your builder or working as your main contractor.

Garden Walls

Boundary walls, retaining walls, raised planters and decorative garden features. We work with reclaimed and new bricks to match the character of your property.

Blockwork & Footings

Foundations and below-ground blockwork for new builds, extensions and outbuildings. We work to engineer's specifications and ensure everything is level and true.

Repointing & Repair

Tired mortar joints bringing your walls down? We repoint in lime or cement mortar, replace damaged bricks and restore walls to their former glory.
