Building Trust, One Brick at a Time

Cornerstone Brickwork was founded by brothers Tom and Phil Reeves in 2003. What started as a two-man operation has grown into one of Nottingham's most respected bricklaying firms, with a team of twelve skilled tradesmen.

We're proud members of the Federation of Master Builders and carry full public liability insurance. Every project we take on receives the same level of care and attention to detail, whether it's a garden wall or a multi-unit development.

What We Do

Comprehensive brickwork solutions for every project

🏠

Residential Extensions

Expert brickwork for single and double storey extensions, orangeries, and conservatory bases. We match existing brickwork seamlessly and work to your architect's specifications.

🏗

New Build Projects

Full brickwork packages for housing developments and individual new builds. We've completed work for several major developers across the Midlands region.

🧱

Walls & Boundaries

Retaining walls, garden boundaries, and feature walls. We work with all brick types and can incorporate decorative bonding patterns into your design.

🔧

Repairs & Repointing

Structural repairs, crack stitching, and complete repointing services. We use traditional lime mortars where appropriate for period properties.
