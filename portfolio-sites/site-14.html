About Whitfield Construction

Whitfield Construction is a York-based building company with over 20 years of experience in residential and commercial construction. Founded by Paul Whitfield in 2004, we've grown from a small team to a well-established company with a reputation for reliability and quality.

We manage projects from start to finish, coordinating all trades and ensuring every build meets the highest standards. We're fully insured, CITB registered, and proud members of the Federation of Master Builders.

Our Services

Comprehensive construction solutions

🏠

Home Extensions

Single and double storey extensions designed and built to transform your living space. We manage the entire project including planning applications, building regulations, and all finishing trades.

🏗

New Build Homes

Complete new build construction from foundations to handover. We work with your architect to deliver bespoke homes built to the highest specifications and energy efficiency standards.

🔧

Renovations & Refurbishments

Full property renovations, including structural alterations, reconfigured layouts, and complete internal refurbishments. We breathe new life into tired properties.

🏛

Loft Conversions

Dormer, hip-to-gable, and Velux loft conversions that add valuable living space. Full structural work, insulation, and finishing to create rooms you'll love.

🏢

Commercial Fit-Outs

Office renovations, shop fit-outs, and commercial building works. We deliver on time and minimise disruption to your business operations.

🛠

Structural Repairs

Subsidence repairs, underpinning, lintel replacement, and structural wall modifications. Fully engineered solutions with professional project management.
