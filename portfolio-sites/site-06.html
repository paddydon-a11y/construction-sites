What We Do

Our Heating & Gas Services

From emergency boiler repairs to full central heating installations, we've got your home covered.

Boiler Installation

New boiler installations from all major brands. We'll survey your home, recommend the right boiler, and install it with a manufacturer-backed warranty of up to 12 years.

Learn more →

Boiler Servicing

Annual boiler servicing to keep your system running efficiently and safely. Required to maintain your manufacturer warranty. Takes around 45 minutes with no mess.

Learn more →

Central Heating

Full central heating system design and installation. Whether you're upgrading an old system or fitting out a new-build, we install radiators, pipework, and controls to suit your home.

Learn more →

Underfloor Heating

Wet underfloor heating systems for extensions, new builds, and renovations. Energy-efficient, invisible, and luxuriously comfortable. We install on both concrete and timber subfloors.

Learn more →

Gas Safety Checks

Landlord gas safety certificates (CP12) and domestic gas safety inspections. We check all gas appliances, flues, and pipework to ensure everything meets current regulations.

Learn more →

Emergency Repairs

Boiler broken down? No heating or hot water? We offer same-day emergency callouts across Liverpool and Merseyside. Most common faults fixed on the first visit.
