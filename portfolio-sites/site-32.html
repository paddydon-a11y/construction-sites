What We Do

From heritage restorations to modern re-roofs, we offer a complete range of roofing services across Lincoln and Lincolnshire.

△

Pitched Roof Repairs

Expert repair and maintenance of pitched roofs including slate, clay, and concrete tile work. Storm damage repairs available at short notice.

■

Flat Roof Systems

Modern flat roofing solutions including GRP fibreglass and EPDM rubber, fully guaranteed for up to 25 years.

✿

Heritage Restoration

Sympathetic restoration of period roofs using traditional materials and techniques. Listed building experience throughout the county.

◊

Lead Work

Skilled leadwork for valleys, flashings, dormers, and church roofs. Sand-cast and machine-rolled lead fitted to the highest standards.

⌆

Chimney Repairs

Chimney repointing, rebuilds, cowl fitting, and flaunching. We ensure your chimney stack is safe and watertight.

◖

Guttering & Rainwater

Replacement and repair of all guttering systems, downpipes, and hoppers in uPVC, aluminium, and traditional cast iron.
