Welcome to The Kitchen Workshop

Founded in 2012 by Mark Townsend, The Kitchen Workshop is an independent kitchen fitting company based in Norwich. We specialise in designing and installing kitchens that combine style, practicality, and lasting quality.

Our small, dedicated team manages every project personally. From the initial design consultation through to the final finishing touches, you'll work directly with the people who care about getting it right. We're fully insured and proud members of the Kitchen Bathroom Bedroom Specialists Association (KBSA).

Free Design Service

Complete Installation
