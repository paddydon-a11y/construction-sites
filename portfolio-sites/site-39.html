Our Services

From concept to completion, we deliver bathrooms that combine beauty with practicality.

◆

Bathroom Design

Bespoke design consultations to create a bathroom that reflects your style. 3D visualisations available so you can see the finished result before we start.

■

Full Installation

Complete bathroom fitting including plumbing, electrics, tiling, sanitaryware, and all finishing touches. One team manages every aspect of your project.

○

Wet Rooms

Walk-in wet room installations with precision tanking, underfloor heating, and premium large-format tiles for a seamless, luxurious finish.

△

Precision Tiling

Expert tiling in porcelain, ceramic, natural stone, and mosaic. Complex patterns, feature walls, and intricate details executed to perfection.

♢

Underfloor Heating

Electric and water-fed underfloor heating systems installed beneath your tiles. Warm, comfortable floors all year round with energy-efficient technology.

❖

En-Suite & Cloakrooms

Clever space planning for en-suites and cloakrooms. We maximise every square inch while maintaining an airy, luxurious feel.
