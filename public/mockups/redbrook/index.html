Our Services

Electrical Services Done Right

From domestic rewires to commercial installations and EV chargers. Gary and the team handle all electrical work across Barnsley and Yorkshire.

Domestic Electrical Installation

Complete electrical installations for new builds, extensions, and renovations. Sockets, switches, lighting, and full house wiring to the latest regulations.

Commercial Electrical Services

Industrial and commercial installations, maintenance, and upgrades. Warehouse lighting, office fit-outs, and commercial premises fully compliant.

EV Charger Installation

Electric vehicle home charging point installation. Fully certified EV charger fitting with all necessary electrical upgrades and safety testing.

EICR Testing & Inspection

Electrical Installation Condition Reports for homeowners and landlords. Thorough testing to identify faults and ensure your property meets safety standards.

Rewiring & Upgrades

Full and partial rewires for older properties. Upgrading outdated wiring to current standards, ensuring your home is safe and compliant.

Fuse Board Replacements

Consumer unit upgrades to modern RCD-protected boards. Replacing old fuse boxes with the latest safety-compliant units, fully tested and certified.

Lighting Design & Installation

Indoor and outdoor lighting installations. LED upgrades, garden lighting, security lighting, and feature lighting to transform any space.

Fault Finding & Repairs

Fast diagnosis and repair of electrical faults. Tripping circuits, dead sockets, flickering lights — Gary tracks down the issue and gets it sorted.

PAT Testing

Portable Appliance Testing for businesses and landlords. Keeping your workplace compliant and your employees safe with regular PAT testing.

Security Lighting

Professional security lighting installation for homes and businesses. Motion-sensor lights, floodlights, and CCTV power supplies for total peace of mind.
