What We Stock

Electrical, Plumbing & DIY Under One Roof

Everything for plumbers, electricians, and DIYers. From a single socket to a full bathroom suite — all at the lowest prices in the Northwest.

Bathroom Suites

Complete bathroom suites, cloakroom suites, basins, toilets, baths, and shower enclosures. Budget to premium ranges, all on display in our showroom.

Boilers & Heating

Combi, system, and regular boilers from Baxi, Navien, and more. Central heating components, cylinders, pumps, thermostats, and controls.

Electrical Supplies

Sockets, switches, wiring, lighting fixtures, consumer units, and electrical accessories. Everything an electrician needs on the job.

Plumbing Tools & Parts

Pipes, fittings, connectors, valves, copper, plastic, sealants, PTFE — all the essentials for any plumbing job, big or small.

Showers & Wetwall Panels

Electric showers, mixer showers, shower heads, trays, and wetwall panels from Mermaid and more. Transform any bathroom or shower room.

Radiators & Towel Rails

Panel, column, and designer radiators in all sizes. Heated towel rails, valves, and TRVs to keep every room warm.

Lighting & Sockets

Indoor and outdoor lighting, LED bulbs, downlights, spotlights, sockets, switches, and dimmer controls for every room.

Taps & Mixers

Kitchen and bathroom taps, mixer taps, monobloc taps, and shower valves. Quality brands at the best trade prices.

DIY & Hardware

General DIY supplies, fixings, screws, brackets, sealants, adhesives, and hardware essentials. Everything to finish the job properly.
