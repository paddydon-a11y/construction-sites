What We Do

Our Services

All domestic and commercial electrical and data work undertaken. From simple socket moves to full new-build projects.

House Rewiring

Complete and partial rewires to bring your home up to current regulations. Minimal disruption with a professional, tidy finish every time.

Fuse Board Upgrades

Consumer unit replacements and upgrades to modern RCD-protected boards. Essential for safety and insurance compliance.

Lighting Design & Installation

Feature lighting, downlights, LED strips, pendants, outdoor and security lighting. From domestic kitchens to commercial bars and restaurants.

EV Charger Installation

Data & Networking
