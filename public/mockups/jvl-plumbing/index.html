Our Services

Complete Plumbing & Heating Solutions

From emergency boiler repairs to full new-build installations. Our Gas Safe registered team covers every aspect of plumbing and heating across Somerset.

Boiler Installation

Expert boiler installations from Vaillant advanced installers. Up to 10 years manufacturer warranty. We help you choose the best boiler to suit your home and budget.

Boiler Repairs

Fast boiler breakdown response across Somerset. We diagnose the fault, source the correct parts, and get your heating back on — often the same day.

Boiler Servicing

Annual boiler servicing to ensure safe operation, maintain efficiency, prevent breakdowns and keep your manufacturer warranty valid. LPG and natural gas.

Central Heating

Radiator and valve replacements, central heating repairs and upgrades, power flushing, and cylinder changes — vented and unvented systems.

Underfloor Heating

Bespoke underfloor heating design and installation tailored to your property. Efficient, even warmth throughout your home with no radiators needed.

Power Flushing

Radiators not warming evenly? Bleeding them regularly? Noisy boiler? A power flush removes all the sludge and restores your heating system's performance.

Bathrooms

Full bathroom installations from start to finish. Taps, showers, suites, and all associated plumbing work. Small repairs to complete refits.

New Builds

Extensive experience in new-build plumbing and heating — from timber frame builds to barn conversions. Full first and second fix covered.

Landlord Gas Safety

Annual landlord gas safety checks and CP12 certificates. Keep your tenants safe and your property compliant with current regulations.
