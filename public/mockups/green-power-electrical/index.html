What We Do

Expert Electrical & Renewable Energy Services

From EV charging and solar panels to boiler installations and heat pumps — Green Power covers residential and commercial clients across Preston and Lancashire.

EV Charge Point Installation

Home and workplace EV charge point installations. Future-proof your property with a professionally fitted and certified charging solution.

Solar PV Panels

Reduce your energy bills with professionally installed solar PV systems. Full design, installation, and certification for homes and businesses.

Electrical Wiring & Rewiring

Complete house rewires and partial upgrades for older properties. Bringing your home electrics up to the latest 18th Edition standards.

Fuseboard Upgrades

Fuse board replacements and upgrades to meet current regulations. Metal consumer units with RCBOs for maximum protection.

Lighting Installation

Interior and exterior lighting design and installation. From LED downlights to garden lighting and smart home solutions.

EICR Testing & Inspection

Electrical inspection condition reports, PAT testing, and landlord safety certificates. 2391 Inspection and Testing certified.

Boiler Installation & Repair

Gas Safe registered boiler installations, servicing, and repairs. NVQ Level 3 qualified in both electrical and plumbing work.

Heat Pump Installation

Air source and ground source heat pump installation. Improve your home’s efficiency with renewable heating solutions.
