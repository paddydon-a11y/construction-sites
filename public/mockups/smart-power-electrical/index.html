What We Do

Expert Electrical Services

From EV charging and solar panels to battery storage and full rewires — Smart Power covers residential, commercial, and blue-chip clients across Greater Manchester.

EV Charger Installation

Home and workplace EV charger installations from all major brands including Tesla, Zappi, and more. Trusted by Tesla Motors as an approved installer.

Consumer Unit Upgrades

Fuse board replacements and upgrades to meet current regulations. Metal consumer units with RCBOs for maximum protection.

Solar Panel Installation

Reduce your energy bills with professionally installed solar PV systems. Full design, installation, and certification for residential and commercial properties.

Full & Partial Rewires

Complete house rewires and partial upgrades for older properties. Bringing your home electrics up to the latest 18th Edition standards.

Battery Storage Systems

Store your solar energy and use it when you need it. We install leading battery storage solutions to maximise your energy independence and savings.

Testing & Certification

EICR electrical inspection reports, PAT testing, and landlord safety certificates. Essential for compliance and property sales.
