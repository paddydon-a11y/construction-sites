What We Do

Our Services

From full rewires to electrical compliance, our NAPIT registered team delivers expert electrical solutions for homes, businesses and industrial premises across the West Midlands.

Domestic Installations

Full and partial rewires, new sockets and switches, consumer unit upgrades, lighting design and all domestic electrical work. Part P certified and fully tested to BS7671 standards.

Commercial Installations

Complete electrical installations for offices, retail units, restaurants and commercial premises. Three-phase systems, distribution boards, emergency lighting and data cabling.

Industrial Installations

Heavy-duty electrical installations for factories, warehouses and industrial units. Motor controls, machinery wiring, containment systems and high-power distribution.

Testing & Inspection

EICR electrical testing, periodic inspections and safety reports for landlords, homeowners and businesses. Full certificates issued on completion with clear, detailed findings.

Electrical Compliance

Specialist electrical compliance for the private rental sector. Mandatory EICR landlord safety certificates, fast turnaround and competitive pricing for portfolio landlords and letting agents.

Outdoor & Garden Lighting

Professional outdoor and garden lighting installations. Pathways, patios, decking, security lighting and feature lighting to transform your outdoor spaces. Weatherproof and energy-efficient.
