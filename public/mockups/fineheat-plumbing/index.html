Our Services

Plumbing & Heating Excellence

From high-end bathroom installations to renewable heating systems. We deliver quality workmanship on every project across Bournemouth and Dorset.

Boiler Installation & Replacement

New boiler installations from leading manufacturers. System upgrades and replacements for improved efficiency and lower energy bills.

Boiler Servicing & Repairs

Annual servicing, fault diagnosis and efficient repairs. Keep your boiler running safely and reliably all year round.

Luxury Bathroom Installation

Complete bathroom design and installation to the highest standard. Walk-in showers, freestanding baths, wall-hung furniture and premium tiling.

Central Heating Systems

Full central heating installation, upgrades and system conversions. Radiator replacements and balancing for even, efficient heat.

Underfloor Heating

Wet underfloor heating design and installation for new builds, extensions and renovations. Even heat distribution with no radiators.

Air Source Heat Pumps

Renewable heating solutions with air source heat pump installation. Lower running costs and reduced carbon footprint for your home.

Unvented Hot Water Cylinders

Supply, installation and servicing of pressurised unvented cylinders for high-performance mains-pressure hot water throughout your home.

Gas Safety Certificates

Landlord gas safety checks (CP12), annual inspections and compliance certification. Fully Gas Safe registered.

Leak Detection & Repairs

Fast, accurate leak detection and professional repairs to minimise damage and get you back to normal quickly.

Power Flushing

Central heating power flush to remove sludge, rust and debris. Restore efficiency and even heat to all your radiators.
