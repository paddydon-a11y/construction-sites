What We Do

Electrical & Renewable Energy Services

From solar panels and battery storage to full rewires and EV chargers — Chris and the team deliver it all.

Solar Panel Installation

MCS-certified solar PV installations for homes and businesses. Reduce your energy bills and carbon footprint with a 10-year workmanship guarantee.

Battery Storage

Store excess solar energy with home battery systems from leading manufacturers like SigEnergy and GivEnergy. Maximise your energy independence.

EV Charger Installation

Home and workplace EV charger installations including Zappi and Tesla Wall Connector. Fully certified to current regulations.

House Rewiring

Full and partial rewires to bring your home up to the latest safety standards. 3-year workmanship guarantee on all electrical work.

EICR & Testing

Electrical Installation Condition Reports for landlords, homeowners and commercial premises. Full compliance certificates issued.

Air Conditioning

Commercial and domestic air conditioning installation and maintenance. Multi-split systems for offices, shops and residential properties.

Fuse Board Upgrades

Consumer unit replacements and upgrades to modern RCD-protected fuse boards. Essential for home safety.

Emergency Electrical

24/7 emergency call-outs for power failures, tripped circuits and urgent electrical faults across Southampton.
