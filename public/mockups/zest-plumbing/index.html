Our Plumbing & Heating Services

From boiler installations to bathroom plumbing and gas safety certificates, Zest Plumbing delivers trusted solutions across Aspull, Wigan and the Northwest.

Boiler Installation & Replacement

Expert installation of new boilers and full replacements of old, inefficient systems. Rob will advise on the best boiler for your home and budget, with Gas Safe certified installation and manufacturer warranties.

Boiler Repairs & Servicing

Fast, reliable boiler repairs and annual servicing to keep your heating running efficiently. Rob diagnoses faults quickly, carries genuine parts and ensures your boiler stays safe and compliant year-round.

Central Heating Systems

Complete central heating installations, radiator fitting and replacement, and powerflushing services to restore circulation and efficiency. Whether it's a full system or a single radiator, Rob has you covered.

Bathroom Plumbing

Professional bathroom fitting, shower installation, leak repairs and pipework. From full bathroom renovations to fixing a dripping tap, Rob delivers clean, reliable plumbing work at honest prices.

General Plumbing

All-round plumbing services covering taps, toilets, pipes and leak repairs. No job is too small — from a dripping tap to a burst pipe, Rob provides fast, effective solutions across Wigan and the Northwest.

Gas Safety Certificates

Landlord gas safety inspections (CP12) and gas safety certificates for homeowners. Fully Gas Safe registered, Rob provides thorough inspections and fast turnaround to keep your property safe and legally compliant.
