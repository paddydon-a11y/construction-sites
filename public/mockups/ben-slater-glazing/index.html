Our Services

Glazing Services in Burnley

From emergency glass repairs to full window installations. Ben handles all domestic and commercial glazing work across Burnley and surrounding areas.

Emergency Glass Replacement

Smashed window? Ben offers same-day emergency glass replacement for homes and businesses. Fast response when you need it most.

Double Glazing

New double glazed units supplied and fitted. Reduce heat loss, cut energy bills, and improve security with modern sealed units.

Window Repairs

Broken locks, hinges, handles, and mechanisms. Misted or blown double glazed units replaced. Get your windows working properly again.

Shopfront Glazing

Commercial shopfront glass installations and repairs. Toughened safety glass, plate glass, and laminated glass for retail premises.

Domestic Glazing

All types of glass for your home. Single glazing, double glazing, patterned glass, safety glass, and toughened glass cut to size.

Security Glazing

Laminated and toughened safety glass for improved security. Board-up and emergency glazing to secure your property fast.

Mirror Cutting & Fitting

Bespoke mirrors cut to size for bathrooms, bedrooms, gyms, and dance studios. Supplied and professionally fitted.

Glass Cut to Size

Any type of glass cut to your exact measurements. Greenhouse glass, table tops, shelves, picture frame glass, and specialist glass.
