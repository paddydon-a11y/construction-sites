Our Story

A Family Business Built on Trust

Craig Kershaw Ltd was founded in 1991 by Craig Kershaw, a Burnley local who built his reputation on quality workmanship and straight talking. For over three decades, the company has been the go-to builder for families, landlords, and Burnley Council alike.

Today, Brad Kershaw leads the team, carrying forward the same values his dad built the business on — honest pricing, reliable timelines, and work you can be proud of. From a single extension to a full social housing contract, we treat every job the same.
