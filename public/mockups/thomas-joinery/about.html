Our Story

Built on Craftsmanship & Pride

Thomas Joinery Constructions Ltd carries forward a family tradition in carpentry spanning over 35 years. What began as a passion for woodworking has grown into a trusted joinery business serving Bedford, Kempston, Shortstown, Cardington, Sharnbrook and the surrounding areas.

We approach every project with diligence and professionalism, using only quality materials sourced from respected producers. Whether it's a custom kitchen, a handcrafted staircase, fitted wardrobes or garden decking, each piece is designed and built to your exact specifications.

Through our established partnerships with electricians, tilers, plumbers, bricklayers and painters & decorators, we're able to offer a comprehensive, end-to-end service. One point of contact, one team dedicated to getting your project completed to the highest standard.
