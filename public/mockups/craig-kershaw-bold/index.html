Our Story

Family Built. Trust Earned.

Craig Kershaw started this company in 1991 with a simple idea: do honest work at honest prices. For over three decades, that's exactly what we've done — building homes, extensions, and community trust across Lancashire.

Today, Brad Kershaw carries on the family legacy with the same values, the same work ethic, and the same commitment to getting it right first time. Whether it's a kitchen extension or a council housing contract, we bring the same care to every job.
