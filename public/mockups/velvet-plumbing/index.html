Our Services

Plumbing & Heating Done Right

From boiler installations to emergency plumbing repairs. Velvet Plumbing & Heating handle everything across Doncaster and South Yorkshire.

Boiler Installation

Full boiler installations including system design, pipework, and commissioning. We fit all major brands and ensure everything is done to the highest standard with Gas Safe certification.

Boiler Repairs & Servicing

Fast boiler breakdown response with 24/7 emergency callouts. Annual servicing to keep your boiler running safely and efficiently. All major brands covered.

Radiators & Central Heating

Radiator installation, replacements, balancing, and power flushing. We keep your central heating system running efficiently throughout every room.

Gas Fires & LPG

Gas fire installation, servicing, and repairs. We also handle LPG systems for properties without mains gas supply. All work Gas Safe certified.

Unvented Hot Water Systems

Installation, servicing, and repair of unvented hot water cylinders. High-pressure hot water delivered to every tap in your home.

General Plumbing

Leak repairs, tap replacements, shower installations, and all general plumbing work. No job too small — fast, reliable service across Doncaster.
