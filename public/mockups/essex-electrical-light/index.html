Our Electrical Services

From design to installation, we provide comprehensive electrical solutions for homes and businesses across Essex.

Electrical Design

Bespoke electrical design and planning for new builds, extensions and renovations. Full specification and compliance documentation.

Commercial Electrical

Full commercial electrical installations for offices, retail units, warehouses and industrial premises. NICEIC certified to the highest standards.

Residential Electrical

Complete domestic rewires, consumer unit upgrades, additional sockets, lighting and all home electrical needs with minimal disruption.

Solar Installation

Professional solar panel installations to reduce your energy bills. Full design, supply, installation and MCS certification included.

EV Charging

Home and commercial EV charger installations. OZEV grant approved installers for all leading charger brands.

Maintenance

Planned and reactive electrical maintenance for domestic and commercial properties. Fast response times and 24/7 emergency call-outs.

Testing & Inspection

EICR testing, periodic inspection reports, PAT testing and full electrical condition assessments for compliance and peace of mind.

Landlord Safety Checks

Mandatory EICR certificates for landlords and letting agents. Fast turnaround to keep your properties legally compliant.

LED Upgrades

Energy-efficient LED lighting upgrades for homes and businesses. Reduce energy costs by up to 80% with modern LED solutions.
