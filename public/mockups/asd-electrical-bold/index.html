What We Do

Our Electrical Services

From domestic rewires to commercial fit-outs, ASD Electrical Services delivers safe, certified electrical work across Chester and Cheshire. Part P registered and fully insured on every job.

Domestic Installations

Full and partial rewires, consumer unit upgrades, additional sockets, lighting circuits and smart home wiring. Clean, professional work in your home every time.

Commercial Installations

Complete commercial electrical solutions for offices, shops and business premises. Minimal disruption, maximum reliability, fully certified.

Data Cabling

Structured data cabling installations for homes and businesses. Cat5e, Cat6, fibre optic and network infrastructure delivered to the highest standards.

Landlord Safety Certificates

Mandatory EICR reports for rental properties. Full electrical installation condition reports, remedial works and certification for landlords and letting agents.

Testing & Inspection

Comprehensive electrical testing and inspection services. EICR reports, periodic testing and full installation condition reports for homeowners and businesses.

Fault Finding & Emergencies

Fast, reliable fault finding and emergency callout service. Tripping circuits, power failures and urgent electrical issues diagnosed and resolved quickly.
