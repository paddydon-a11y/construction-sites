Our Heritage

A Reputation Many Years in the Making

The original Corlett Electrical business was founded on 2nd April 1892 when George Corlett became sole proprietor. Mr Corlett was involved in a foundry, and provided early street lighting in Wigan.

The modern company, Corlett Electrical Engineering Co (1981) Ltd, has continued that tradition of excellence. With directors who started at entry-level and have over 50 years of combined experience, we invest in annual apprenticeships and maintain excellent staff retention at all levels.
