Our Services

Electrical Services for Every Job

From a single socket to a full commercial fit-out. Whatever the job, we've got you covered.

Domestic Electrical Installation

Full house wiring, extensions, new builds, and renovations. All work certified to BS 7671 standards.

Commercial Electrical Services

Office fit-outs, retail units, warehouses, and industrial premises. Minimal disruption, maximum reliability.

Rewiring & Upgrades

Complete house rewires, partial rewires, and electrical upgrades. Bringing older properties up to current standards safely.

Fuse Board Replacements

Consumer unit upgrades and replacements to meet current 18th Edition regulations. Keeping your property safe and compliant.

EICR Testing & Inspection

Electrical Installation Condition Reports for landlords, homeowners, and commercial properties. Fast turnaround, clear reports.

Fault Finding & Repairs

Tripping circuits, flickering lights, dead sockets. We diagnose and fix electrical faults fast so you're not left in the dark.

Lighting Design & Installation

Kitchen downlights, garden lighting, feature lighting, and LED upgrades. We design and install lighting that transforms your space.

EV Charger Installation

Home and workplace EV charging points. OZEV-approved installer. Future-proof your property with a professional installation.

Fire Alarm & Emergency Lighting

Design, installation, and maintenance of fire alarm systems and emergency lighting. BS 5839 compliant for commercial and residential properties.

Alarm Systems & Security

Intruder alarm installation, CCTV, and security systems for homes and businesses. Full design, installation, and maintenance.
