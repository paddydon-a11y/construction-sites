Our Services

Plumbing Services Done Right

From dripping taps to boiler relocations. Joel handles everything plumbing across Wolverhampton and the surrounding areas.

Leak Repairs

Leaking pipes, joints, taps, or toilets — Joel finds the source fast and fixes it properly. Emergency callouts available for flooding and burst pipes.

Toilet Repairs & Replacements

Faulty flush mechanisms, leaking cisterns, or full toilet replacements. Joel diagnoses the issue and gives honest advice on whether to repair or replace.

Tap Replacements

Dripping or broken taps replaced quickly and cleanly. Kitchen taps, bathroom taps, mixer taps, and isolator valves fitted at a reasonable cost.

Boiler Services & Relocation

Boiler servicing, repairs, and full relocations. Joel moved a boiler from one end of a kitchen to the other and the workmanship was brilliant.

Radiators & Central Heating

Radiator valve replacements, new radiator installations, bleeding, and full central heating diagnostics. No job too small — even a single valve.

General Plumbing

Shower pumps, hot water issues, kitchen plumbing, bathroom refurbs — whatever the job, Joel gets it sorted quickly and professionally.
