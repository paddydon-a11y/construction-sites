Our Services

Electrical Services Done Right

From fuse board upgrades to full rewires and EV charger installations. NICEIC approved, fully qualified, and all work guaranteed.

Fuse Board Upgrades

Modern consumer unit installations to bring your property up to current safety standards. Full certification and testing included.

EICR Testing

Electrical Inspection Condition Reports for homeowners and landlords. Thorough testing to identify faults and ensure compliance with BS7671 regulations.

House Rewiring

Full and partial rewires for older properties. New cabling, sockets, lighting circuits, and consumer units — all completed to the highest standard.

EV Charger Installation

Domestic and commercial EV charging point installations. Professional setup for all major charger brands, with full certification.

Outdoor Power & Sockets

Weatherproof outdoor sockets, garden lighting, and external power supplies. Perfect for sheds, outbuildings, and garden entertainment areas.

Cooker Installations

Electric cooker and hob installations with dedicated circuits. Safe, certified installations that meet all current wiring regulations.
