Our Services

Electrical Services Done Right

From fuse board upgrades to EV charger installations, John delivers reliable electrical work across Reading and Berkshire. BS7671 compliant and fully guaranteed.

Fuse Board Upgrades

Modern consumer unit installations to current regulations. Replacing outdated fuse boards with RCD-protected units for improved safety, ensuring your home meets the latest BS7671 standards.

EICR Testing

Electrical Installation Condition Reports for landlords, homeowners, and businesses. Thorough inspection and testing of your electrical systems with certified documentation.

House Rewiring

Full and partial rewires carried out with minimal disruption. Bringing older properties up to current safety standards with neat, professional cabling throughout every room.

EV Charger Installation

Domestic, commercial, and workplace EV charging point installations. From single home chargers to multi-bay workplace solutions, fully certified and ready to charge.

Outdoor Power & Sockets

Weatherproof outdoor sockets, garden lighting, and external power supplies. Perfect for sheds, workshops, hot tubs, and outdoor entertainment areas -- safely installed and protected.

Fault Finding & Repairs

Expert electrical fault diagnosis and repair. Tripping circuits, flickering lights, dead sockets -- John will trace the issue and fix it properly, not just patch over the problem.

Cooker Installations

Electric cooker and oven connection and installation. Dedicated circuits installed to manufacturer specifications, with the correct cable rating and proper isolation switching.

General Electrical Work

Additional sockets, light fittings, dimmer switches, smoke alarms, and more. Whatever the electrical job, no matter the size, it'll be completed to the highest standard.
