What We Do

Our Services

From full house rewires to commercial installations, NT Electrical provides expert electrical solutions across Chesterfield, Sheffield and surrounding areas. NICEIC certified and trusted since 2009.

Full & Part Rewires

Complete and partial house rewires to the latest 18th Edition wiring regulations. From Victorian terraces to modern builds, we upgrade your electrics safely and efficiently with minimal disruption.

Inspection & Testing

Comprehensive EICRs (Electrical Installation Condition Reports) for homeowners, landlords and businesses. Full condition reports with clear findings, recommendations and certificates issued on completion.

Consumer Unit Upgrades

Upgrade your old fuse box to a modern consumer unit with RCD protection. Essential for safety and insurance compliance. All upgrades are NICEIC certified and compliant with current regulations.

Landlord Safety Certificates

Mandatory EICR landlord safety certificates for rental properties. Fast turnaround, competitive pricing and full compliance with current legislation. Trusted by landlords and letting agents across Derbyshire.

LED & Security Lighting

Energy-efficient LED upgrades for homes and businesses. Exterior security lighting, floodlights, PIR sensors and garden lighting. Reduce your energy bills and improve safety with modern lighting solutions.

Home Automation

Smart home installations including automated lighting, heating controls, smart switches and integrated home systems. We bring your home into the future with reliable, easy-to-use technology solutions.
