Our Electrical Services

From full rewires to smart home automation, NT Electrical delivers NICEIC-certified solutions across Chesterfield, Sheffield and North Derbyshire.

Full & Part Rewires

Complete and partial house rewiring for properties of all ages. Whether you have a Victorian terrace or a modern build, we bring your electrics up to the latest BS 7671 standards with minimal disruption.

Inspection & Testing (EICRs)

Comprehensive Electrical Installation Condition Reports for homeowners, landlords and businesses. We identify potential hazards, ensure compliance and provide detailed documentation you can trust.

Consumer Unit Upgrades

Replace your old fuse box with a modern, regulation-compliant consumer unit featuring RCD protection. A vital upgrade that protects your family from electric shock and reduces fire risk.

Landlord Safety Certificates

Fast, reliable EICR testing for landlords and letting agents across multiple properties. We keep your rental portfolio legally compliant with quick turnaround times — trusted by Ashgate and Derbyshire County Council.

LED & Security Lighting

Energy-efficient LED lighting design and installation for homes and businesses. Plus professional security lighting, floodlights and motion-sensor systems to keep your property safe and well-lit.

Home Automation

Smart home electrical installations including automated lighting, smart switches, programmable heating controls and integrated home systems. Bring your property into the future with intelligent electrical solutions.
