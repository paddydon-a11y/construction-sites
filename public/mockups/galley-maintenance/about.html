Our Story

Professional Service, Every Time

Galley Maintenance Ltd is a professional handyman and property maintenance company based in Broughton, Milton Keynes. We provide a comprehensive range of services from electrical installations and lighting to full kitchen and bathroom refits.

Our team covers all trades — electrical, plumbing, carpentry, painting and decorating, flooring, landscaping and more. Whether it’s a small repair or a complete room renovation, we bring the same level of professionalism and attention to detail to every job.

We believe in doing things right the first time. Quality materials, clean workmanship and reliable service are at the heart of everything we do.
