Our Services

Plumbing & Heating Services

From boiler installations and radiator fitting to full bathroom refurbishments — Anthony Bradley covers all your plumbing and heating needs. Fair prices, quality workmanship.

Boiler Installation

New boiler installations from trusted manufacturers. Professional fitting, full commissioning, and all paperwork handled. Quoted and fitted quickly at a fair price.

Boiler Servicing & Repair

Annual boiler servicing to keep your system running efficiently and safely. Fast fault diagnosis and repair when things go wrong.

Radiator Installation

New radiator fitting, replacements, and upgrades throughout your home. We’ll help you choose the right size and style for every room.

Bathroom Refurbishment

Complete bathroom transformations — from modern refurbs to full redesigns. Tiling, plumbing, fixtures, and fittings all taken care of.

Leak Repairs

Fast response for leaks and plumbing emergencies. We’ll find the source of the problem and get it sorted quickly to prevent further damage.

Central Heating Systems

Full central heating installations and upgrades. From new systems to extending existing heating into additional rooms. Efficient, reliable warmth.

Plumbing Repairs

General plumbing repairs and maintenance. Taps, toilets, stop taps, pipework, and more. No job too small — reliable service at fair prices.

Gas Safety Checks

Landlord gas safety certificates and domestic gas checks. Keeping your property compliant and your family safe. Full reporting provided.

Power Flushing

Professional power flushing to clear sludge and debris from your central heating system. Restore efficiency and heat output to your radiators.
