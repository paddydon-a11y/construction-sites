What We Stock

Everything Plumbing & Heating Under One Roof

From a single tap washer to a full bathroom suite — we stock it all at competitive trade prices. Professional tradespeople and DIY customers welcome.

Boilers

Gas and oil boilers from leading manufacturers. Combi, system, and regular boilers in stock. Expert advice on the right boiler for your property.

Radiators

Wide range of radiators, towel rails, and valves. Panel, column, and designer styles in various sizes to suit every room.

Bathroom Suites

Complete bathroom suites, shower enclosures, baths, basins, and toilets. Budget-friendly to premium ranges, all on display in our showroom.

Plumbing Tools & Parts

Pipes, fittings, connectors, valves, sealants, PTFE, copper, plastic — all the essentials for any plumbing job, big or small.

Heating Supplies

Central heating components, cylinders, pumps, motorised valves, thermostats, and controls. Everything to keep heating systems running.

Guttering & Drainage

Complete guttering systems, downpipes, drainage fittings, and soil pipes. Plastic and cast iron options for domestic and commercial use.

Taps & Showers

Kitchen and bathroom taps, mixer taps, shower heads, shower valves, hoses, and accessories. All leading brands stocked.

DIY & Decorating

Painting and decorating supplies, plastering materials, sealants, adhesives, and general DIY essentials. Everything under one roof.

Fixings & Sundries

Screws, brackets, clips, plugs, sealant tapes, and all the fixings and sundries you need to finish the job properly.
