Our Services
Sash Window Specialists
Expert care for Glasgow's traditional windows. We restore, repair and upgrade sash and case windows to the highest standard — preserving heritage while improving performance.

Sash & Case Window Restoration

Full restoration of traditional wooden sash and case windows. Returning your windows to their original glory while dramatically improving performance.

Draught Proofing

Eliminate cold draughts with discreet brush-pile draught seals fitted into your existing sash windows. Dramatically warmer rooms without losing character.

Double Glazing Upgrades

Retrofit slim-profile double glazing into your original sash frames. All the energy efficiency benefits of modern glass without replacing your heritage windows.

Sash Window Repair

Rotten timber, broken cords, stuck sashes, damaged glazing bars — we repair rather than replace wherever possible, saving you money and preserving originals.

Sash Cord & Weight Replacement

Broken sash cords and missing weights are the most common sash window problem. We replace cords and balance weights so your windows glide smoothly again.

Window Painting & Finishing

Professional preparation, priming and painting of all timber windows. Quality paints that protect your windows from the Scottish weather for years to come.

Timber Window Installation

New bespoke timber sash and case windows crafted to match the original style of your property. Perfect for replacements where restoration isn't viable.

Emergency Window Repairs

Broken glass, jammed windows, security concerns — we respond quickly to make your windows safe and secure. Fast, reliable emergency service across Glasgow.
