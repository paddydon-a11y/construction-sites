What We Do

Our Electrical Services

Comprehensive electrical solutions for homes, businesses and industrial premises across Wolverhampton and the West Midlands. All work NAPIT certified and BS7671 compliant.

Domestic Installations

Full and partial rewires, consumer unit upgrades, additional sockets, lighting installations and smart home wiring. Clean, tidy work every time.

Commercial Installations

Complete commercial electrical solutions from office fit-outs to retail and hospitality. Minimal disruption, maximum reliability.

Industrial Installations

Heavy-duty electrical work for factories, warehouses and industrial units. Three-phase installations, distribution boards and power systems.

Testing & Inspection

EICR reports, PAT testing and full electrical installation condition reports. Certified documentation for landlords, buyers and insurers.

Electrical Compliance

Specialist electrical compliance for the private rental sector. Mandatory EICR certificates, remedial works and full regulatory compliance for landlords.

Outdoor & Garden Lighting

Transform your outdoor space with professionally installed garden lighting, security lights, pathway lighting and decorative exterior features.
