About Us

Built on Family Values

Craig Kershaw Ltd was founded in 1991 by Craig Kershaw, who built the company's reputation on quality work and honest pricing. Today, Brad Kershaw carries forward those same values — leading a skilled team across Lancashire and West Yorkshire.

From a kitchen extension to a full council housing contract, we bring the same care and attention to every project. That's why homeowners and Burnley Council keep coming back.
