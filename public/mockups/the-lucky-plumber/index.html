What We Do

Plumbing Services That Set the Standard

From routine maintenance to complex installations, The Lucky Plumber covers every aspect of residential and commercial plumbing across South London.

Emergency Leak Repair

Burst pipe at 3am? We're there. Rapid 24/7 response across all South London boroughs. We locate, isolate and repair leaks fast to minimise damage to your property.

Boiler Installation

Full boiler installations from leading manufacturers. System design, pipework, commissioning, and Gas Safe certification. Finance options available on all new boilers.

Boiler Servicing & Repairs

Annual servicing to keep your boiler running safely and efficiently. Fast breakdown response for all major brands including Worcester, Vaillant, Baxi, and Ideal.

Leak Detection

Advanced leak detection technology to find hidden water leaks without unnecessary demolition. Thermal imaging and acoustic detection for precision results.

Underfloor Heating

Professional underfloor heating design and installation for new builds and renovations. Efficient, invisible warmth that transforms your living spaces.

Power Flushing

Restore your central heating system's performance with a professional power flush. Removes sludge, rust, and debris for better heat output and lower energy bills.

Drain Unblocking

Blocked drains, toilets, sinks, and sewers cleared quickly using professional-grade equipment. CCTV drain surveys available for persistent drainage issues.

Bathroom Installation

Complete bathroom design and installation. From luxury suites to compact ensuites, we handle every element: plumbing, tiling, electrics, and finishing touches.

Pipe Replacement

Old lead or corroded pipes? We replace entire plumbing systems or individual sections with modern copper or plastic pipework. Clean, tidy work guaranteed.
