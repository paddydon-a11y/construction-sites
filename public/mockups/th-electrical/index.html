Our Services

Domestic, Commercial & Industrial

NICEIC approved electrical contractors offering a complete range of services across Huddersfield, Holmfirth and the surrounding areas. No job too big or small.

Full House Rewires

Complete domestic rewiring to BS 7671 standards. We ensure minimal disruption so you can comfortably stay in your home throughout the work.

Outdoor & Garden Lighting

Stunning exterior lighting design and installation. Pathway lights, uplighters, feature lighting and security floodlights to transform your outdoor space.

Commercial Electrical

Electrical installations, maintenance and testing for offices, shops, warehouses and commercial premises. Fully NICEIC approved.

24 Hour Emergency Call-Outs

Round-the-clock emergency electrician service. Power failures, tripping circuits, dangerous wiring — we're available when you need us most.

Electrical Repairs & Fault Finding

Expert fault diagnosis and efficient repairs. From flickering lights to complete circuit failures, we'll find and fix the problem fast.

Consumer Unit Upgrades

Fuse board replacements and consumer unit upgrades to current regulations. Improved safety with RCD protection throughout your property.

EICR & Electrical Inspections

Electrical Installation Condition Reports, periodic inspections and landlord safety certificates. Full compliance documentation provided.

PAT Testing

Portable appliance testing for landlords, businesses and commercial premises. Certificates provided for compliance and insurance purposes.

Smoke & Fire Alarms

Smoke alarm and fire detection system installation, testing and maintenance. Hardwired systems for reliable, always-on protection.

Sockets, Switches & Extras

Additional sockets, USB outlets, light switches, external power points and data cabling. Small jobs completed to the same high standard.
