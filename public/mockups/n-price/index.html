Our Services

Plumbing & Heating in Brighton

From emergency repairs to full bathroom installations, N Price delivers professional plumbing, heating, and gas services across Brighton, Hove, and East Sussex.

Boiler Installation

New gas boiler installations with top brands. Expert fitting, full warranty, and Gas Safe certification on every job.

Boiler Servicing & Repair

Annual boiler servicing, breakdown repairs, and diagnostics. Keep your boiler running efficiently and safely all year round.

Central Heating

Full central heating installation, repairs, and upgrades. Radiator fitting, system balancing, and power flushing.

Bathroom Installation

Complete bathroom design and fitting, from luxury suites to practical en-suites. Tiling, plumbing, and finishing all handled.

Gas Safety Checks (CP12)

Gas Safe registered inspections for landlords and homeowners. Certificates issued on the day. Competitive rates.

Emergency Plumbing

Burst pipes, leaks, and boiler breakdowns dealt with fast. N Price respond quickly when you need them most.

Air Source Heat Pumps

ASHP installation, servicing, and repair. Energy-efficient heating solutions for a greener home.

Gas Cooker Installation

Safe, certified installation of gas cookers, hobs, and range cookers. Disconnection and disposal of old appliances available.

Leak Detection & Repairs

Fast diagnosis and repair of leaks, dripping taps, and faulty pipework. Minimising damage and getting things fixed right.

General Plumbing

Tap replacements, toilet repairs, waste disposal, pipework, and all general plumbing work for homes and businesses.
