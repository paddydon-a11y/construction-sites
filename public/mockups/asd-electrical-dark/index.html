What We Do

Our Services

From domestic rewires to commercial fit-outs, Andrew and the team deliver expert electrical solutions across Chester and Cheshire. Part P registered, fully insured and always to the highest standard.

Domestic Installations

Full and partial rewires, new sockets, switches, consumer unit upgrades, lighting design and all household electrical work. Part P certified and tested to the latest wiring regulations.

Commercial Installations

Complete electrical installations for offices, retail units, restaurants and commercial premises. Three-phase systems, distribution boards, emergency lighting and power infrastructure.

Data Cabling

Structured data cabling for homes and businesses. Cat5e, Cat6 and fibre optic installations, network points, patch panels and server room wiring. Clean, tested and future-proof.

Landlord Safety Certificates

Mandatory EICR landlord safety certificates for rental properties. Fast turnaround, competitive pricing and full compliance with current legislation. Essential for landlords and letting agents.

Testing & Inspection

Comprehensive electrical testing and periodic inspections for homeowners, landlords and businesses. Detailed condition reports with clear findings and recommendations. Full certificates issued.

Fault Finding & Emergencies

Rapid fault diagnosis and emergency electrical callouts across Chester and Cheshire. Tripping circuits, power loss, burning smells — we identify the problem quickly and fix it safely.
