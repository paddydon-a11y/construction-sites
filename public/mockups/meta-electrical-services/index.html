Full & Partial Rewires

Complete house rewiring or targeted upgrades to bring older properties up to current regulations. Minimal disruption, maximum safety.

Consumer Unit Upgrades

Replace outdated fuse boxes with modern RCD-protected consumer units. Essential for safety and insurance compliance.

EICR Testing
