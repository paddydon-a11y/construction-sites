The Art of Kitchen Design

Stone & Oak Kitchens was established in 2014 by interior designer Alexandra Mortimer and master craftsman William Stone. Together, they bring a unique combination of design sophistication and traditional woodworking excellence to every project.

Based in Reading, we work with homeowners across Berkshire, Oxfordshire, and the wider Thames Valley. Every kitchen we create is entirely bespoke, designed around your space, your lifestyle, and your aesthetic. We use only the finest materials - solid hardwoods, natural stone, and premium hardware - to create kitchens of enduring beauty.

Our Services

A bespoke approach to luxury kitchen design

📐

Bespoke Design

Comprehensive design service including home consultation, mood boarding, material selection, 3D rendering, and detailed technical drawings.

🪜

Handmade Cabinetry

Custom cabinetry crafted in our Reading workshop from solid hardwoods. Dovetail joints, hand-painted finishes, and meticulous quality control.

⬜

Natural Stone Worktops

Marble, granite, and quartzite worktops sourced from the finest quarries. Templated and fabricated to our exacting standards.

🛠

Full Installation

Expert installation by our own skilled fitters. Plumbing, electrics, tiling, and final finishing all managed in-house for seamless results.

💡

Lighting Design

Architectural lighting schemes that enhance the beauty and functionality of your kitchen. Integrated, ambient, and task lighting perfectly balanced.

🔧

Premium Appliances

Supply and integration of luxury appliances from Gaggenau, Miele, Sub-Zero, and Wolf. Expert specification and installation.
