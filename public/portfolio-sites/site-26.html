Caring For Cambridge's Gardens Since 2014

Willowbrook Gardens provides reliable, high-quality garden maintenance for private homes, rental properties and small commercial premises across Cambridge and the surrounding villages. Whether you need regular upkeep or a one-off tidy, our friendly team takes genuine pride in every garden we look after.

What We Do

Comprehensive garden maintenance and care services

🌿

Regular Maintenance

Weekly, fortnightly or monthly garden visits including mowing, edging, weeding, pruning and general tidying to keep your garden in top shape.

🌳

Hedge Trimming

Professional hedge cutting and shaping for all species and sizes. We keep your boundaries neat and your hedges healthy throughout the growing season.

🍂

Lawn Care

Mowing, feeding, scarifying, aerating and overseeding. Seasonal lawn treatment programmes tailored to your grass type and conditions.

✎

Planting & Borders

Seasonal planting, border redesign, mulching and plant care advice to add colour, structure and interest to your garden beds.

🍁

Autumn & Winter Tidy

Leaf clearance, cutting back perennials, protecting tender plants, and preparing your garden for the colder months ahead.

🔨

One-Off Garden Clearance

Overgrown garden rescue, green waste removal, stump grinding and site clearance. We'll bring any garden back from the brink.
