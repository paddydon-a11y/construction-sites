Rooted in the Landscape

Meadow Lane Landscapes is a family-run garden design practice based just outside Worcester. We specialise in creating naturalistic gardens that work with the local landscape, soil and climate rather than against them. Our approach is simple: we listen, we design, and we build gardens that bring genuine joy to the people who use them. From cottage gardens to contemporary outdoor living spaces, every project begins with understanding what matters most to you.

What We Do

Complete garden design and landscaping services

🌿

Garden Design

Bespoke planting plans and layout designs that suit your garden's aspect, soil type and the way you live outdoors.

■

Patios & Terracing

Natural stone and porcelain patios, raised terraces and step features for sloping gardens throughout Worcestershire.

💧

Ponds & Water Features

Wildlife ponds, formal water features and rill channels to add movement and tranquillity to your garden.

🌳

Planting Schemes

Expertly chosen plants for seasonal interest, low maintenance and wildlife value. Native and ornamental species blended beautifully.

🔨

Garden Structures

Pergolas, arbours, raised beds and bespoke timber structures designed to complement your garden's character.

Lawn & Turf
