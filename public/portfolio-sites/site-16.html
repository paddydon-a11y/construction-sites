Powering Glasgow Since 2008

Volt Electrical Services was founded by Craig Morrison, a time-served electrician with a passion for delivering safe, high-quality electrical work. Based in Glasgow, we serve domestic and commercial clients across the Central Belt.

We're SELECT approved, fully insured, and registered with NICEIC. Every job we complete comes with full certification and a satisfaction guarantee. Our team of ten qualified electricians handles everything from a single socket to a complete commercial installation.
