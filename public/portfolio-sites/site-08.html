Services

Internal Plastering

Complete internal plastering services including skim coats, bonding, multi-finish and specialist Venetian plaster. We work across both new-build and renovation projects, ensuring a smooth and durable finish to walls and ceilings throughout.

External Rendering

Silicone, monocouche and traditional sand-and-cement rendering for residential and commercial exteriors. Our renders provide lasting weather protection whilst enhancing your property's appearance with a clean, contemporary or textured finish.

Decorative Coving

Installation of fibrous plaster coving, cornicing and ceiling roses. We carry an extensive range of period and contemporary profiles, and can produce bespoke moulds to match existing detailing in listed and heritage buildings.

Dry Lining

Plasterboard fixing and dry lining for partitions, wall linings, and ceiling systems. Fast-track solutions for commercial fit-outs and residential developments, with expert taping and jointing for a seamless ready-to-decorate surface.

About the Practice

Precision Plastering & Rendering was founded in Edinburgh in 2009 by master plasterer Ewan MacAllister. With over two decades of experience across Scotland's finest properties, we have built a reputation for meticulous attention to detail and an uncompromising commitment to quality.

Our small, dedicated team of time-served plasterers specialises in both traditional lime plasterwork and modern finishing techniques. Whether restoring ornate Georgian cornicing in the New Town or delivering seamless skim finishes in a contemporary apartment, we bring the same care and precision to every surface we touch.

We believe plastering is as much about craft as it is about construction. The quality of a plastered wall or ceiling defines the character of a room, and that responsibility is something we take seriously on every single project.
