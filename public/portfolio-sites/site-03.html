25 Years on the Rooftops

Since 1999, NorthPoint Roofing has been Newcastle's most trusted name in roofing. Founded by brothers David and Mark Patterson, we've grown from a two-man team to a 30-strong workforce — but our commitment to quality craftsmanship has never wavered.

Every project is managed personally by our directors. We don't use subcontractors, and we never cut corners. That's why 80% of our work comes from repeat customers and referrals.
