Our Process

01

Consultation

We visit your home to discuss ideas, measure your space and understand how you use your kitchen day to day.

02

Design

Our designers create detailed 3D renders so you can visualise your new kitchen before a single cabinet is ordered.

03

Installation

Our fitters work methodically and tidily, keeping disruption to a minimum. Most kitchens are completed within two weeks.

04

Aftercare

We return to check everything is perfect and provide a full aftercare package for your peace of mind.
