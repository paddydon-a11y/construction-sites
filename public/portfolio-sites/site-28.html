Decorating with Care & Precision

Colour & Co Decorating is a husband-and-wife team based in Durham. We specialise in interior and exterior painting, wallpapering and decorating for homeowners and landlords across the North East. With over a decade of experience, we bring a meticulous eye for detail to every project — from a single feature wall to a full house redecoration. We protect your furnishings, prepare surfaces properly and leave every room looking its absolute best.
