The Oakwood Difference

Oakwood Kitchens was founded in 2009 by husband-and-wife team Daniel and Sophie Clarke. Daniel brings over 20 years of joinery experience, while Sophie handles the design side, creating stunning kitchen layouts that are both beautiful and practical.

Based in Cheltenham, we serve clients across the Cotswolds and Gloucestershire. Every kitchen we create is designed around your lifestyle, fitted by our own skilled team, and backed by our personal guarantee of quality.

What We Offer

A complete kitchen service from design to installation

📐

Kitchen Design

Free home design visits with 3D visualisation. We work with you to create the perfect layout, choose materials, and plan every detail before work begins.

🛠

Full Installation

Complete kitchen fitting by our own experienced team. Carpentry, plumbing, electrics, tiling, and decorating all managed under one roof.

🪜

Bespoke Joinery

Custom-made cabinetry, islands, and storage solutions crafted in our workshop. Solid timber, hand-painted, and built to last a lifetime.

⬜

Worktops & Surfaces

Supply and fitting of granite, quartz, solid timber, and composite worktops. We template, fabricate, and install to a perfect finish.

💡

Kitchen Lighting

Integrated LED under-cabinet lighting, pendant installations, and ambient schemes. We design lighting that enhances the look and function of your kitchen.

🔧

Appliance Supply

We source and install premium kitchen appliances from leading brands including Bosch, Neff, and Siemens. Trade pricing passed directly to you.
