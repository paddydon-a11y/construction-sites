About Waterline

Waterline Bathrooms & Tiling was founded in 2015 with a simple goal: to bring honest, high-quality bathroom fitting to homeowners across the Bournemouth area. Our small but dedicated team handles every project personally, ensuring the same high standards from start to finish. We believe that a beautiful bathroom shouldn't cost the earth, and we work hard to deliver exceptional results at fair, transparent prices.
