About Cathedral Scaffolding

Founded in 2004 and based in the heart of Salisbury, Cathedral Scaffolding has grown from a two-man operation into one of Wiltshire's most respected access companies. We combine traditional values of hard work and integrity with modern safety standards and equipment. Our team of CISRS-qualified operatives takes pride in delivering clean, safe scaffolding on every project, from a single chimney stack to a full cathedral encapsulation.
