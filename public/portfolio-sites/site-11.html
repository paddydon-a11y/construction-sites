About Redfern Bricklaying

Founded in 1998, Redfern Bricklaying has built a solid reputation across Sheffield and the surrounding areas. We're a family-run business that takes genuine pride in every course of bricks we lay.

Whether you need a new garden wall, an extension, or a complete new build, our experienced team delivers craftsmanship that stands the test of time. We're fully insured, NHBC registered, and committed to exceeding your expectations.

Our Services

Professional bricklaying services tailored to your needs

🧱

House Extensions

Single and double storey extensions built to the highest standards. We work closely with architects and project managers to deliver seamless results.

🏠

New Builds

Complete brickwork packages for new residential and commercial builds. We handle everything from foundations to the ridge line.

🧱

Garden Walls

Boundary walls, retaining walls, and decorative garden features. Built to complement your outdoor space and built to last.

🔧

Repointing

Expert repointing of tired mortar joints to restore the appearance and weatherproofing of your brickwork. All joint profiles matched.

🏗

Commercial Work

Industrial and commercial brickwork projects of all scales. Reliable timekeeping and professional site management guaranteed.

🏛

Restoration Work

Sympathetic restoration of period brickwork using traditional techniques and lime mortars. Heritage projects a speciality.
