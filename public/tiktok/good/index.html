What We Do

Our Services

From full house rewires to EV charger installations, our NICEIC approved team delivers expert electrical solutions for homes across Manchester and Greater Manchester.

Full & Part Rewires

Complete and partial house rewires carried out to the highest standards. We upgrade outdated wiring systems, ensuring your home meets current BS7671 regulations with minimal disruption.

Consumer Unit Upgrades

Modern consumer unit (fuse board) upgrades with RCD protection. We replace outdated fuse boxes with the latest safety standards, fully tested and certified on completion.

Testing & EICRs

Electrical Installation Condition Reports for landlords, homeowners and businesses. Thorough inspection and testing with detailed findings, clear certificates and fast turnaround.

CCTV & Security

Professional CCTV and security system installations to protect your home or business. HD cameras, smart monitoring, motion sensors and complete security solutions with remote viewing.

Lighting Design

Transform any space with expert lighting design and installation. From recessed downlights to feature lighting, outdoor illumination and smart lighting controls for the modern home.

EV Charger Installation

OZEV-approved electric vehicle charger installations. We supply and fit all major brands with fast, professional installation — typically within a few days of booking.
