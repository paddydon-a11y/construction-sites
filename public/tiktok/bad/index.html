About Us

Sparks Electrical Services have been serving the Manchester area since 2008. We are a family run business run by Dave and his team. We offer a freindly and reliable service at competative prices. No job is too big or too small! We cover all areas of Manchester and surounding areas including Salford, Stockport, Bolton, Oldham and Bury.

We are fully qualifed and insured electricains. All our work is carried out to the highest standars and complies with current BS 7671 regulations. We are Part P registerd and can certify all work carried out.

Dave has over 15 years experiance in the electrical trade and prides himself on providing a quailty service.
